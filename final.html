The path isn’t hard because of the work — it’s hard because the roadmap isn’t built for them.

Slide 4 • Broken System

Slide 4

Bruno: Alex isn’t struggling because he lacks drive or talent — he’s being let down by a system that leaves him to figure things out by himself. He’s surrounded by information, but none of it is presented in a way that gives him confidence or clarity. And when he finally finds guidance, it’s often too generic and not meaningful, never quite matching his current situation or what his next steps forward should be. So of course he feels stuck. With so much noise and no personalized direction, Alex is left overwhelmed, second-guessing himself, and unsure of what to do next.

Shift blame from the individual to how the system is designed. TEAM • Panday

NIKITA: To fix this problem, we built Panday. Rather than telling you how it works, let us show you.

Clean handoff into the live demo section. TEAM • Panday
Demo-part1 • NIKITA

Demo-part1

NIKITA: When you first open Panday, you’re greeted with an onboarding survey. This lets you share where you’re at in your specific journey — your current apprenticeship level, which path you’re on, and whether you’re a Canadian citizen or not — so Panday can tailor the roadmap to you. After you’re done with the onboarding, you're greeted with a quick 60-second tutorial. These yellow nodes represent your main career milestones – Level 1, Level 2, all the way to Red Seal. Unlike a generic checklist, this is a visual map of your entire journey. Each milestone has three types of support: - Blue (Resources) – learning materials, study guides, external links to verified sources - Green (Actions) – tasks you need to complete to progress through your journey - Orange (Roadblocks) – common challenges and how to overcome them If you click on any of the connectors, every milestone breaks down into specific, trackable tasks. You can mark things off to make a mental note of what you’ve completed and what you still need to do. As you complete items, the progress fills in visually on the map. And if anything is unclear, you can always ask Panday AI for more clarification.

Walk through onboarding, tutorial, nodes, support types, and progress. TEAM • Panday
Demo-part2 • DARREL

Demo-part2

DARREL: Okay, this says I need 1,500 hours of work experience before Level 2... but I'm doing a mix of residential and commercial work. Does all of that count? How does this apply to my specific situation?

Pose the realistic "how does this apply to me?" question. TEAM • Panday
Demo-part3 • Bruno & DARREL

Demo-part3

Bruno: Let us ask Panday, our AI companion, in the chatbox. PROMPT #1: “Do residential hours count toward my apprenticeship?” Notice how it cites exactly where this information comes from — you can hover to preview the exact section from the source. And if you’re ever too lazy to type, you can always use the voice prompt. DARREL: I'm a Level 1 apprentice working for a small contractor. I do about 60% residential, 30% commercial, and some light industrial. My employer isn't sure what to log for my hours. Can you break down exactly what counts and what documentation I need to track? Bruno: As you can see, our AI knows Alex is at Level 1, knows he's a BC resident, and pulls from the official program documentation to give a personalized answer — not generic advice you'd find on a random forum.

Show AI clarification with a simple question and full-context voice prompt. TEAM • Panday
Demo-part4 • Ozem

Demo-part4

Ozem: What if Alex is in a circumstance that is only specific to him? Panday's already got him covered with a custom node feature. Send the prompt now mofucka. To showcase this, let's suppose that Alex is required to track his hours and monthly employer reviews. He asks Panday AI: “Create a reminder for me to log my hours every Friday and do monthly reviews with my employer.” Panday AI proposes a custom node with the title: “Track Work Hours”, and it renders a checklist of to-dos to keep Alex on track with tasks. This will be attached to Level 1. As you can see, Panday is built to adapt to everyone’s needs, supplying you with personalized nodes that remedy your unique situations. CHECK ALL CHECKLIST NODES AND CONTINUE TO LEVEL 2. Once you complete everything in your current node, you are able to proceed to the next level of your trades journey in BC.

Highlight custom nodes, personalization, and progressing to the next level. TEAM • Panday
